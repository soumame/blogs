## What is Hack Club

> Hack Club is a network run by a nonprofit organization that operates programming and maker schools (hands-on making) for high school students around the world. As of November 2024, Hack Club has many participants worldwide — over 80,000 — and every week thousands of students work on a wide variety of projects.

https://hackclub.com

- Active on Slack
- Targeted at under-18s
- They occasionally hold events, and you may receive various freebies or perks
