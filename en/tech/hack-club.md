## What is Hack Club

> Hack Club is a network run by a nonprofit organization that operates programming and maker clubs (hands-on making) for high school students around the world. Hack Club currently has many participants worldwide (as of November 2024), totaling over 80,000 people, and each week thousands of students work on a variety of projects.

https://hackclub.com

- Active on Slack
- Aimed at people under 18
- They occasionally host events and sometimes give out various items
