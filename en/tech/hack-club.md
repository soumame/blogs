## What is Hack Club

> Hack Club is a network run by a nonprofit organization that operates programming and maker schools (hands-on making) for high school students around the world. Hack Club currently has many participants worldwide (as of November 2024), numbering over 80,000, and every week thousands of students work on a wide variety of projects.

https://hackclub.com

- Active on Slack
- For those under 18
- They occasionally host events where participants can receive various things
