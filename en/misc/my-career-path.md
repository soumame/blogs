## I don't know what I should do
I'm currently attending university, but there are times when I don't know what I should do.

### Focus on one thing or be broad and shallow?
Until last year I was trying to master one thing — I was doing software development. That's how I got into university.

But recently I've been doing things like hardware, and my topics have become scattered.

## What should I do from now on?
