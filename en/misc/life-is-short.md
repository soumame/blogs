20 years is 7,300 days.
Even as an adult, I still haven't lived 10,000 days.
In the computer world, 10,000 is such a tiny number.
Even if you live 80 years, it's less than 30,000 days.
What's more, more than one-third of that time is spent sleeping.
How long will I continue to grow?
