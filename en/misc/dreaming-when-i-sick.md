The moment I woke from the dream, I still had the memory, and while half-asleep I apparently recorded what I remembered as a voice memo. Looking back later, it was such a bizarre text that it felt creepy, but since it was something I wouldn't normally be able to write, I'm recording it here.

## Main text (original)
I went to the hospital and had a craniotomy.

I don't know what was wrong with me, but I went to the hospital, they opened my head, and performed a surgery that used electrical stimulation—applying electricity—to adjust my brain activity so that it would become more advantageous and superior.

They told me that if I imagined the flame exactly as instructed, it would transfer into my visual field, so I just had to keep doing that. The images were really strangely and beautifully connected, and it felt odd. However, after seeing about two of the images, an overwhelming sense of fatigue came over me. In the end I stopped halfway. Is that okay?
