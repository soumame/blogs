Obsidian is incredibly convenient.

## What is Obsidian?
- The basic functionality is the same as Notion, and you write using [[markdown|Markdown]]
- Notion stores data in a hierarchical way, while Obsidian structures data through links between notes
- To be frank, Notion does have more features, but Obsidian runs locally & is noticeably lighter than Notion
	- It was perfect for me — I couldn't fully utilize Notion's features & wanted specific features or an API
- You can control the appearance and behavior with themes and plugins developed by the community
