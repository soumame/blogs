Obsidian is incredibly convenient.

## What is Obsidian
- The basic features are the same as Notion, and you write using [[markdown|Markdown]]
- Notion stores data in a hierarchical way, while Obsidian structures data through links between notes, etc.
- Honestly, Notion has more features, but Obsidian runs locally and is clearly lighter than Notion
	- It was perfect for me, since I can't make full use of Notion's features and I wanted certain features or an API
- You can control the appearance and behavior with themes and plugins developed by the community
