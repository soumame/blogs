Obsidian is incredibly useful.

## What is Obsidian
- Its basic functionality is the same as Notion's, and you write using [[markdown|Markdown]].
- Notion gives the impression of storing data hierarchically, while Obsidian structures data through links between notes.
- Frankly, Notion has more features, but Obsidian runs locally & is noticeably lighter than Notion.
	- It was perfect for me, who couldn't fully utilize Notion's features & wanted specific features or an API.
- You can control the appearance and behavior with community-developed themes and plugins.
