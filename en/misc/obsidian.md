Obsidian is incredibly convenient.

## What is Obsidian?
- The basic functionality is the same as Notion, and you write using [[markdown|Markdown]].
- Notion stores data in a hierarchical way, whereas Obsidian structures data via links between notes.
- To be honest, Notion has more features, but Obsidian runs locally and is noticeably lighter than Notion.
	- I couldn't fully make use of Notion's features & I wanted specific functions or APIs, so Obsidian was perfect for me.
- You can control appearance and behavior with themes and plugins developed by the community
