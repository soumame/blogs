Obsidian is incredibly convenient.

## What is Obsidian
- The basic features are the same as Notion, and you write using [[markdown|Markdown]]
- Notion has the image of storing data hierarchically, while Obsidian structures data through links between notes
- To be honest, Notion has more features, but Obsidian runs locally & is clearly lighter than Notion
	- It was perfect for me, who can’t fully utilize Notion’s features & wants specific features or APIs
- Appearance and behavior can be controlled with themes and plugins developed by the community
