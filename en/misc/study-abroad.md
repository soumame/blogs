Lately, I haven't really thought hard about the reason, but I've been feeling like I want to go abroad.

When I reconsider it, I started wondering why I want to go.

Originally, I spent about five years abroad and then came back.

Now I'm attending a university in Japan, and I've been thinking I want to go abroad again — even as an exchange student would be fine.

And when I think again about the motivation, it's not really a deep reason.

Maybe it's because going abroad seems cool? I don't really know the reason. When I glance at things online, people who go abroad often seem to be successful. Since I was actually able to change a lot myself, I wondered if it would have a positive effect. Also, because I can speak English to some extent, I started to think, what's the point of staying in Japan?

But when I think about it, I don't really know the meaning of staying in Japan either; I could also ask, what's the point of going abroad? So I'm kind of unsure.

Also, people hype up foreign universities as amazing — you can learn cutting-edge things. Some say Japanese education is outdated or whatever, but when I actually look into it, it doesn't feel like that at all. There's so much I don't know.

I don't know.
