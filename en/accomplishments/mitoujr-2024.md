## What is Mitou Junior
> A program that supports elementary, junior high, and high school students (and [technical college students](https://www.mext.go.jp/a_menu/koutou/kousen/index.htm)) aged 17 and under who have original ideas and exceptional technical skills.
> There is a program called the [Mitou Project](https://www.ipa.go.jp/jinzai/mitou/koubo/), a human-resource development program for people under 24 implemented by the Information-Technology Promotion Agency (an independent administrative institution under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry) ([IPA](https://www.ipa.go.jp/)). **Mitou Junior** was established and is operated mainly by alumni of that Mitou Project.
> Mitou Junior started in 2016, and to date has received **860 applications and supported and selected 136 creators**. - [What is Mitou Junior - Mitou Junior](https://jr.mitou.org/about)
