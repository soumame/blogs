## What is Mitou Junior
> It is a program that supports elementary, junior high, and high school students, as well as [technical college students](https://www.mext.go.jp/a_menu/koutou/kousen/index.htm) (kosen), aged 17 and under who have original ideas and outstanding technical skills.
> There is the [Mitou Project](https://www.ipa.go.jp/jinzai/mitou/koubo/), a human resource development program for young people aged 24 and under implemented by the Information-technology Promotion Agency (IPA) — an independent administrative institution under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry — and **Mitou Junior** is established and operated mainly by alumni of that Mitou Project (IPA).
> Mitou Junior started in 2016, and to date has received **860 applications and supported/selected 136 creators**. - [未踏ジュニアとは - 未踏ジュニア](https://jr.mitou.org/about)
