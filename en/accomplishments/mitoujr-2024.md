## About Mitou Junior
> It is a program that supports elementary, junior high, and high school students and [kosen students](https://www.mext.go.jp/a_menu/koutou/kousen/index.htm) aged 17 or younger who have original ideas and outstanding technical skills.
> There is a program called the [Mitou Project](https://www.ipa.go.jp/jinzai/mitou/koubo/), a human resource development program for young people aged 24 and under implemented by the Information-technology Promotion Agency (IPA), an independent administrative institution under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry, and **Mitou Junior** was established and is operated mainly by graduates of that Mitou Project.
> Mitou Junior started in 2016, and to date has received **860 applications and has supported/selected 136 creators**. - [What is Mitou Junior - Mitou Junior](https://jr.mitou.org/about)
