## What is Mitou Junior
> A program that supports elementary, junior high, and high school students, as well as [kosen students](https://www.mext.go.jp/a_menu/koutou/kousen/index.htm), who are 17 years old or younger and possess original ideas and outstanding technical skills.
> [The Mitou Project](https://www.ipa.go.jp/jinzai/mitou/koubo/) is a human resources development program for young people aged 24 and under implemented by the Information-technology Promotion Agency ([IPA](https://www.ipa.go.jp/)), an independent administrative institution under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry, and **Mitou Junior** was established and is operated mainly by alumni of that Mitou Project.
> Mitou Junior started in 2016, and to date has received **860 applications and has supported/selected 136 creators**. - [What is Mitou Junior - Mitou Junior](https://jr.mitou.org/about)
