## What is Mitou Junior
> It is a program that supports elementary, junior high, and high school students, as well as technical college students ([technical college students](https://www.mext.go.jp/a_menu/koutou/kousen/index.htm)), aged 17 and under who have original ideas and outstanding technical skills.
> The Mitou Program ([未踏事業](https://www.ipa.go.jp/jinzai/mitou/koubo/)) is a talent-development program for young people aged 24 and under run by the Information-technology Promotion Agency ([IPA](https://www.ipa.go.jp/)), an independent administrative agency under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry (METI). **Mitou Junior** was established and is operated mainly by graduates of that Mitou Program.
> Mitou Junior started in 2016, and to date it has received **860 applications and supported/selected 136 creators**. - [What is Mitou Junior - Mitou Junior](https://jr.mitou.org/about)
