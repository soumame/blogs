A world created by Team Ippanjin, a group of members ranging from elementary to high school students who collaborate online, won the Encouragement Award at the Minecraft Cup regional tournament held in Japan.

The Encouragement Award is given at the regional tournament—which features teams that advanced past the preliminaries—to recognize teams expected to do well in the future.
