A world created by Team Ippanjin, a team composed of members ranging from elementary to high school students who operate online, received the Encouragement Award at the Minecraft Cup regional competition held in Japan.

The Encouragement Award is given at regional competitions, where teams that passed the preliminaries compete, to recognize teams expected to show promise in the future.

Two years have passed since the [[en/accomplishments/minecraftcup-2021|previous tournament]], and this was another attempt. Creating a work and submitting it under such tight time constraints is impressive in itself, but the quality has improved markedly compared with two years ago.

Although, for various reasons, we were unfortunately unable to advance to the national tournament, this was a significant turning point that led to subsequent activities.
