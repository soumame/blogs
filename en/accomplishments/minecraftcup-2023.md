A world created by Team Ippanjin, a team composed of members ranging from elementary to high school students who are active online, won the Encouragement Award at the Minecraft Cup regional tournament held in Japan.

The Encouragement Award is given at regional tournaments—where teams that passed the qualifiers compete—to recognize teams expected to achieve further success in the future.
