A world created by Team 逸般人 (Ippanjin), a group of members ranging from elementary to high school students who operate online, won the Encouragement Award at the Minecraft Cup regional competition held in Japan.

The Encouragement Award is given at the regional competition—which features teams that passed the preliminaries—to teams expected to do well in the future.
