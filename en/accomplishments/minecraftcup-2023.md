The world created by Team Ippanjin, a group of members ranging from elementary to high school students who are active on the internet, received an award at the Minecraft Cup regional tournament held in Japan.

The Encouragement Award is given at regional tournaments — where teams that passed the preliminaries compete — to recognize teams that are expected to do well in the future.
