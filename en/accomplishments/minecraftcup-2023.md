A world created by Team Ippanjin, a team made up of members ranging from elementary to high school students who are active on the Internet, received an Encouragement Award at the Minecraft Cup regional tournament held in Japan.

The Encouragement Award is given at regional tournaments—where teams that passed the qualifying rounds compete—to recognize teams that show promise for future success.

Two years have passed since the [[en/accomplishments/minecraftcup-2021|Previous Tournament]], and this was another attempt. It's already impressive that they produced a work and submitted it under such tight time constraints, and compared to two years ago the quality has improved significantly.

Due to various circumstances, they unfortunately did not advance to the national tournament, but this was a major turning point that has led to their subsequent activities.
