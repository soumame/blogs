A world created by Team Ippanjin, a group of members ranging from elementary to high school students who operate online, received the Encouragement Award at the Minecraft Cup regional tournament held in Japan.

The Encouragement Award is given at the regional tournament, which features teams that advanced from the preliminary round, to recognize teams expected to do well in the future.

Two years have passed since the [[en/accomplishments/minecraftcup-2021|previous tournament]], and this was another attempt. They managed to create and submit an entry under very tight time constraints, which is impressive on its own, and the quality has improved significantly compared to two years ago.

Due to various circumstances, they unfortunately did not advance to the national tournament, but this marked a major turning point that has led to their subsequent activities.
