[[minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary to high school age. Participants use Minecraft: Education Edition to create works based on a theme and compete with entries from across the country and overseas.

I competed with a few friends in a competition that tested Minecraft skills and problem-solving ability, and we were finalists and received the Impress "Children and IT" award.

As finalists, we were also interviewed.

https://minecraftcup.com/2185/

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it wouldn't be an exaggeration to say it was the starting point for my 2024 [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|Mitou Junior]]. I couldn't leave home because of the pandemic, and it started as a small chance to participate with friends 😆

As of 2025, as a "graduate"?, I'm helping out in various ways with the organizing body [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Making Council (DMCouncil)]]. I help out and show up at events held around the Kanto area, so please look for me.
