[[en/misc/minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary through high school that uses Minecraft: Education Edition to create works based on themes and compete by accepting submissions from across Japan and overseas.

I competed with a few friends in a competition testing Minecraft technical skills and problem-solving, and we were finalists and received the Impress Kids and IT Award.
