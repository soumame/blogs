[[minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary to high school age. Using Minecraft: Education Edition, participants create works based on a theme and compete by submitting entries from across Japan and overseas.

Together with a few friends, we competed in a contest testing Minecraft skills and problem-solving ability, and were selected as finalists and received the Impress "Children and IT" Award.

As finalists, we also received media coverage and were interviewed.

https://minecraftcup.com/2185/

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it’s not an exaggeration to say that this is where the opportunity for [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|MITOU Junior]] 2024 began. It was a small trigger — because of the pandemic I couldn’t leave home, so I participated with friends 😆

As of 2025, I’m helping out with the organizing body, the [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Making Council (DMCouncil)]], as a sort of alumnus. I help at and attend events held around the Kanto area, so please try to find me there.
