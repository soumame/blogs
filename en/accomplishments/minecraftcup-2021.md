[[en/misc/minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary to high school, where participants use Minecraft Education to create projects following a theme and compete with entries submitted from across Japan and overseas.

I, along with a few friends, entered a competition to test our Minecraft technical skills and problem-solving, and we were finalists and received the Impress "Kids and IT" Award.

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it's not an exaggeration to say that it was the catalyst for my 2024 [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|Mitou Junior]] involvement. It started as a small reason — I couldn't leave home during the pandemic and participated with friends 😆

As of 2025, as an alumnus? I help out in various ways with the organizing body, the [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Manufacturing Council (DMCouncil)]]. I assist at events held around the Kanto area and make appearances, so please look for me.
