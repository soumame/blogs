[[minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary through high school, where participants use Minecraft: Education Edition to create works based on a theme and compete with entries from across Japan and overseas.

I competed with a few friends in a competition that tested Minecraft technical skills and problem-solving, and we were finalists and received the Impress 'Children and IT' Award.

As finalists, we were also interviewed.

https://minecraftcup.com/2185/

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it's no exaggeration to say that it was the starting point for my 2024 [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|Mitou Junior]] entry. It started as a small opportunity—due to the pandemic I couldn't leave home, and I participated together with friends 😆

As of 2025, as a sort of alumnus, I help out with various tasks at the organizing body, the [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Making Council (DMCouncil)]]. I help out and make appearances at events held around the Kanto area, so please look for me if you attend.
