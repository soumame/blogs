[[en/misc/minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary to high school age and below, where participants use Minecraft Education to create works based on a theme and compete with entries from across the country and overseas.

Together with a few friends, we competed in a contest that tested Minecraft skills and problem-solving; we were finalists and received the Impress "Kids and IT" award.

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it's not an exaggeration to say that this was the catalyst for my 2024 [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|Mitou Junior]]. It started as a small opportunity — because of the pandemic I couldn't leave home, and I joined together with friends 😆

As of 2025, as an alum(?) I help out with various things at the organizing body, the [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Monozukuri Council (DMCouncil)]]. I assist at events held around the Kanto area and make appearances, so please look for me.
