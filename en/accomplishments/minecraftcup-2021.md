[[en/misc/minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary through high school. Participants use Minecraft Education to create works based on a theme and compete with entries from across Japan and overseas.

I competed with a few friends in a competition testing Minecraft skills and thinking ability, and we were finalists and received the Impress "Children and IT" award.

As finalists, we also received media coverage and interviews.

https://minecraftcup.com/2185/

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it's not an exaggeration to say that it led to my 2024 [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|MITOU Junior]] opportunity. It started as a small trigger — I couldn't leave home during the pandemic, and I participated with friends 😆

As of 2025, as a graduate? I'm helping out with the organizing body, the [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Monozukuri Council (DMCouncil)]]. I assist at events held around the Kanto area and often make appearances, so please look for me.
