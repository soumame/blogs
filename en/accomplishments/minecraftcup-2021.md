[[minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary through high school, where participants use Minecraft Education to create works based on a theme and compete by submitting entries from across Japan and overseas.

I competed with several friends in a competition to test our Minecraft technical skills and problem-solving abilities; we were finalists and received the Impress Kids and IT Award.

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it wouldn't be an exaggeration to say that it was the starting point for my 2024 [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|Mitou Junior]]. During the pandemic, unable to leave home, it was a small opportunity—joining with friends—that led to this 😆

As of 2025, as a graduate? I help out in various ways with the organizing body, the [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Monozukuri Council (DMCouncil)]]. I assist at events held around the Kanto area and sometimes make appearances, so please try to find me.
