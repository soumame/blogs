[[en/misc/minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary to high school, where participants use Minecraft Education to create projects based on a theme and compete with entries submitted from across Japan and abroad.

Together with a few friends, we competed in a contest that tested Minecraft skills and problem-solving abilities; we were finalists and received the Impress "Children and IT" award.

As finalists, we were interviewed as well.

https://minecraftcup.com/2185/

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it's not an exaggeration to say that the trigger for the 2024 [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|Mitou Junior]] came from here. During the pandemic, unable to leave home, it was a small opportunity of participating with friends 😆

As of 2025, as a kind of alumnus, I help out with various tasks at the organizing body, the [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Manufacturing Council (DMCouncil)]]. I assist and drop by events held around the Kanto area, so please look for me if you attend.
