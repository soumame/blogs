[[minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary to high school, where participants create theme-based works using Minecraft: Education Edition and compete by submitting entries from across Japan and overseas.

I participated with a few friends in a competition to test Minecraft technical skills and problem-solving, and we became finalists and won the Impress Kids and IT Award.

As finalists, we were also interviewed.

https://minecraftcup.com/2185/

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it's not an exaggeration to say that this was the starting point for the 2024 [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|Mitou Junior]]. During the pandemic, unable to leave home, it was a small opportunity when I joined with friends 😆

As of 2025, as a graduate? I help out in various ways with the organizing body, the [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Making Council (DMCouncil)]]. I help out and make appearances at events held around the Kanto area, so please look out for me.
