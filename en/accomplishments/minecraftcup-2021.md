[[minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital making contest held in Japan for children from elementary school through high school, using Minecraft: Education Edition to create works around a theme and compete with entries from across Japan and overseas.

Together with a few friends, we competed in a contest testing Minecraft skills and problem-solving; we were finalists and received the Impress "Children and IT" Award.

As finalists, we also received media coverage and were interviewed.

https://minecraftcup.com/2185/

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it's no exaggeration to say it was the starting point for my involvement in [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|Mitou Junior 2024]]. Because of the pandemic I couldn't leave home, and it began as a small thing — participating with friends. 😆

As of 2025, as a graduate? I'm helping out in various ways with the organizing body, the [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Manufacturing Council (DMCouncil)]]. I help out at and attend events held around the Kanto area, so please look for me if you attend.
