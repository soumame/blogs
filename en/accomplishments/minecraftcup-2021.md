[[minecraftcup|Minecraft Cup]] is a digital creation contest held in Japan for children from elementary through high school, in which participants use Minecraft: Education Edition to create theme-based works and compete with entries from across the country and overseas.

I competed with a few friends in a competition that tested Minecraft skills and problem-solving abilities; we were finalists and received the Impress 'Children and IT' award.

Participating in the Minecraft Cup was a major turning point for me. In particular, it would not be an exaggeration to say that this was the starting point for my 2024 [[en/accomplishments/mitoujr-2024|Mitou Junior]] involvement. It began as a small opportunity — I couldn't leave home because of the pandemic, and I joined together with friends 😆

As of 2025, I'm helping out in various ways as an alumnus? with the organizing body, the [[en/works/dmcouncil|Digital Manufacturing Council (DMCouncil)]]. I help out at events held around the Kanto area and sometimes show my face, so please look for me.
